Welcome to the Department of Mathematical Sciences

The Department of Mathematical Sciences at Kannur University is a center for research and education in mathematics. We offer a range of programs and foster a vibrant academic environment. Our faculty are engaged in cutting-edge research across various mathematical disciplines.

Explore our website to learn more about our activities, faculty, programs, and resources. We are committed to academic excellence and the advancement of mathematical knowledge.
