M.Sc. Mathematics Programme

The M.Sc. Mathematics program at the Department of Mathematical Sciences, Kannur University, is a comprehensive two-year postgraduate course spread across four semesters. It is designed to provide students with an advanced understanding of various branches of pure and applied mathematics.

The curriculum focuses on developing advanced knowledge in areas such as algebraic structures, real and complex analysis, topology, functional analysis, differential equations, and more. The program aims to enhance mathematical reasoning, problem-solving skills, and the ability to construct rigorous mathematical proofs.

Eligibility for admission typically requires a B.Sc. Degree in Mathematics with a minimum of 50% marks. Admission is generally based on the marks secured in the qualifying degree examination.
